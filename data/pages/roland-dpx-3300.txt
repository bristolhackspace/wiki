====== Roland DPX-3300 Flat-Bed Pen Plotter ======

  * A1 size
  * HPGL (Hewlett-Packard Graphics Language)
  * 8 pen
  * Serial (RS-232) and parallel interfaces
  * Made in about 1987
  * Electrostatic paper hold-down

Git repository of useful HPGL-generating software, in C for Linux:

[[http://github.com/anachrocomputer/vec]]
